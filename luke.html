Luke

Luke is a six-year-old white cat; he is very energetic and likes to play fetch. Luke has one brother named Jesse. Luke’s favorite thing to eat are chicken cat treats. Luke is very badly behaved. He often jumps up on furniture and drinks out of people’s water glasses. He also steals people’s jewelry and hides it in his bed. Luke knows several commands; he can sit, jump, follow, play fetch, and give people his paw.
